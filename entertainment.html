Explore Our Entertainment

In addition to the unique shopping experience, the entertainment at the Minnesota Renaissance Festival has been the starting point for many nationally known performers. The magic and comedy act of Penn and Teller got their start at the Minnesota Renaissance Festival. Now The Danger Committee, who began as The Dew Drop Jugglers, has risen to the top and has been seen on America's Got Talent!
